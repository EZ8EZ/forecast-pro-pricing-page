Frequently asked questions

What is included with my subscription?

All annual subscriptions include the software license, 12 months of maintenance, technical support, and all updates released during your subscription period.

Can I upgrade from FP 100 to Extended or TRAC?

Yes. You can upgrade at any time. Contact our sales team and we'll apply your existing investment toward the higher edition.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes. Both Forecast Pro Extended and TRAC offer volume pricing - the more users you add, the lower the per-user cost. Contact sales for a custom quote.

What happens after my subscription renews?

Your renewal covers continued access to the software, support, and all updates released during your subscription period. Contact sales for renewal pricing details.
